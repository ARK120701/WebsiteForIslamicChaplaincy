Mission Statement

Why Muslim Chaplaincy Matters

Muslim chaplains serve people of all faiths and none, offering compassionate spiritual care rooted in professional chaplaincy and informed by the Islamic tradition. Their presence enriches interdisciplinary care teams, strengthens culturally responsive care, and helps ensure that spiritual support reflects the diverse communities they serve.

The National Muslim Chaplains Association (NMCA) exists to advance the profession of Muslim chaplaincy through education, mentorship, professional development, and collaboration. We prepare and support Muslim chaplains across healthcare, higher education, corrections, hospice, the military, and community settings, helping cultivate spiritually grounded, clinically prepared, and ethically committed leaders who serve with excellence and compassion.

Our Goals

Our Pillars

Education

Provide accessible education on Clinical Pastoral Education (CPE), professional certification, and the pathways to a career in chaplaincy — equipping aspiring Muslim chaplains with the knowledge to begin and grow in the profession.

Mentorship

Connect aspiring and early-career Muslim chaplains with experienced professionals who provide guidance, encouragement, and practical insight throughout their professional journey.

Advancement

Increase the presence of qualified Muslim chaplains across healthcare, higher education, corrections, hospice, the military, and community settings through education, advocacy, and professional development.

Community

Build a national network of Muslim chaplains and CPE students so that practitioners across the country are connected, resourced, and mutually supported.

Resources

Develop practical tools, guides, templates, research, and educational materials that support Muslim chaplains, students, institutions, and communities.

Why CPE?

The Gold Standard of Spiritual Care

Clinical Pastoral Education (CPE) is the nationally and internationally recognized standard for professional spiritual care and education. Developed over decades and supervised by accredited educators, CPE trains chaplains to provide compassionate, evidence-informed spiritual support in the most demanding environments.

For Muslim communities, CPE is the gateway to recognition, credentialing, and employment as a professional chaplain. It is not just training — it is the foundation of a lifetime of service.
